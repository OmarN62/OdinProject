BBQ Ribs Recipe

Description of Ribs

BBQ ribs are a smoky, tender masterpiece, dripping with rich, tangy barbecue sauce that caramelizes perfectly as they’re cooked low and slow. The way the meat falls off the bone and melts in your mouth is pure satisfaction, balancing savory, sweet, and sometimes spicy flavors in every bite. Whether slathered in a bold, peppery sauce or a sweeter honey glaze, ribs are the centerpiece of any backyard cookout or barbecue feast. They’re my favorite food because they’re indulgent, messy, and utterly delicious—a treat that feels like a celebration every time, bringing a sense of joy and nostalgia with every bite.

  • How to make BBQ Ribs - The Recipe:
  • 2 racks of pork ribs (baby back or spare ribs)
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp paprika
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper (optional, for spice)
  • 1 cup barbecue sauce (store-bought or homemade)

    • Steps

    1. Prep the Ribs
    2. Make the Dry Rub
    3. Season the Ribs
    4. Bake the Ribs (Low & Slow)
    5. Grill or Broil for Finish
    6. Serve
